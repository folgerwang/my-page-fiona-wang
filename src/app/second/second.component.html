Violin
violin is my main instrument. I know how to play the piano, but not very good at it. I started playing the keyboard as my “study-music” instrument. Then I chose the violin as my main instrument. I have played the violin for 5 years, I am commited to play more. I learned the piano along the way, I can also play the flute. Last year I was part of the Camerata orchestra at GSYO as the associate concertmaster and this year I moved up to Galbraith Honor strings as part of the first violins. I devote 2hrs of my Mondays going to rehearsal and practice throughout the week. My favorite instrument is still the violin though!
